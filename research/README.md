# Research

Source materials, threat intelligence, and reference artifacts that inform the design without being part of the deliverable.

Examples of what belongs here:

- Citizen Lab and Amnesty Security Lab forensic reports cited in the threat model
- Notes from conversations with potential pilot users or partner organizations
- Surveys of adjacent products (Signal, Briar, SimpleX, etc.) — feature comparisons, protocol notes
- CVE writeups for vulnerabilities relevant to the design (Pegasus, Graphite, GrayKey, etc.)
- Notes on cryptographic primitives, post-quantum migration paths, library options
- Hardware notes (Pixel teardowns, Titan M2 documentation, LoRa hardware comparisons)

Items here are reference material, not project artifacts. They inform the work in `../docs/` and `../specs/` but are not the deliverable.
