# Archive — development-process artifacts

> [!NOTE]
> **Frozen historical material.** These are point-in-time development artifacts —
> the adversarial design reviews that informed the design brief and the decision
> records ([`../decisions/`](../decisions/)). They are preserved for provenance
> but are **not maintained**: their internal links may be stale and they describe
> earlier states of the project. For current state, see
> [`../../README.md`](../../README.md), [`../design-brief.md`](../design-brief.md),
> [`../implementation-status.md`](../implementation-status.md), and
> [`../decisions/`](../decisions/).

## Contents

- `section-1-review.md` … `section-10-review.md` (incl. `sections-6-7-review.md`,
  `sections-8-9-review.md`) — per-section multi-lens adversarial reviews of the
  design brief.
- `architecture-simplification-review.md` — the five-lens
  architecture-simplification review.
- `reviews/` — the per-section review-lens outputs, the external-read prompt
  templates and their findings, and the consolidated triage
  (`reviews/external-reads-consolidated.md`) that drove the Sprint 1–3 brief
  revisions.

Other early development artifacts — the initial design-conversation handoff, the
project-name brainstorm, and the storage / network-transport research notes —
were removed from the working tree during the docs cleanup and remain available
in git history.
